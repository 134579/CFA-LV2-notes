acquisition method
将子公司的Asset,Liablility全部加入母公司报表，母公司Cash减少，Equity增加minority interest少数股东权益

P花费800收购S的80%股份，
	P's B/S
	Asset															Liablility
	cash -= 80%(S's equity)											liability += S's liability
	asset += S's asset
	-> asset = P's asset + 100% S's asset - 80%(S's equity)			-> liability = P's liability + 100% S's liability 
			 = P's asset + 20% S's asset + 80% S's liability
																	
																	MI = 20% S's equity
																		
																	Equity
																	equity += 0
													
																	-> equity = P's equity 
	两边依然是平的

	1. 去掉母公司invest那项
	2. 加上子公司asset,liability
	3. 计算goodwill(GAAP:full GW, IFRS:full or partial GW)
	4. 计算mi
