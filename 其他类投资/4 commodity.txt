1. 分类
	energy: 能源, crude oil, 原油
	grains: 谷物
	industry metal: 工业金属
	precious metal: 金, 银, 铂金
	livestock: 牲畜
	softs(cash corps): 经济作物 

2. future 理论
	backwardation: 贴水, future price < spot price
	contango: 升水, future price > spot price
	
	insurance theory: should backwardation
		大部分都是期货的卖方, 所以期货价格下跌
	hedging pressure: backwardation or contango depends on hedging power of long short
	storage theory:  should contango
	
3. 投资future收益
	spot return: 期货合约价格变动带来的收益
		
	roll return: 转仓的收益和损失
		老合约到期, 要买新合约时
		backwardation: cause roll return > 0
		contango: cause roll return < 0
		
	collateral return: 保证金利息收入
